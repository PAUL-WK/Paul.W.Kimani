About Me

With expertise in GIS and Earth Observation (EO), I analyze, visualize, and develop geospatial solutions that bring clarity to complex challenges. I turn location data into powerful insights.


From satellite imagery to interactive mapping applications, my work is about bridging data and decision-making. I thrive on innovation, using technology to transform raw geospatial information into real-world impact. Every project is an opportunity to explore, learn, and push the boundaries of what’s possible with spatial data.

Evapotranspiration Trend Analysis Tool

The ETM App is an interactive Google Earth Engine tool for monitoring global trends in evapotranspiration, precipitation, and productivity. It visualizes annual and monthly data, supports map-based exploration, and aids in assessing water use sustainability in croplands. .


Status:

Completed

Flood Monitoring Tool

The Flood Monitoring Tool uses Google Earth Engine to analyze Sentinel-1 radar data before and after a user-defined flood date. It detects flooded areas within a drawn AOI, calculates the inundated area, and visualizes precipitation trends on an interactive map.


Status:

Completed

Glacier Monitor

Glacier Monitor is a web app that shows glacier retreat over time using satellite images.It compares past and recent imagery with a visual index (NDGI) and creates time-lapse GIFs to highlight changes.


Status:

Completed

Phytoplankton Tracker

This Google Earth Engine app visualizes chlorophyll-a reflectance from MODIS-Aqua data to monitor phytoplankton. It features an interactive map, time-series charts for chlorophyll and sea surface temperature, and a color-coded legend.


Status:

Completed

Soil Erosion Monitoring System

Soil Erosion Monitoring System is an interactive Earth Engine-based web app that monitors soil degradation. It analyzes Sentinel-2 imagery to compute RUSLE-based erosion risk, bare soil frequency, and sustainability factors.


Status:

Completed

Crop yield Estimation
